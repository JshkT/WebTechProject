How the program works

ReeTech recognizes the need for clear and simple guidance when working with participating collection sites. The program provides a variety of support materials and has a team ready to provide assistance to you every step along the way. ReeTech acts as the middleman in facilitating the entire recycling process, from on-boarding, to collection, all the way through the disposition of the collected electronic waste.

To join the collection program, click on the button below and sign up using the form that follows. Alternatively just contact us and a ReeTech member will guide you through each step.
